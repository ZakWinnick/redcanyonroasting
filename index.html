Our Story
It started with a truck and a question.

Red Canyon started with an idea: roast coffee in the back of an electric truck, powered by the truck itself. The roasting moved indoors, but the spirit stayed the same.

Today we source from East African highlands and Hawaiian volcanic soil, roast in small batches, and serve at every Rangeway Basecamp across the country.
