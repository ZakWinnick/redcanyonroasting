Our Story

Born from the
open road.

Red Canyon started with an idea: roast coffee in the back of a Rivian, powered by the truck itself. The name comes from the paint color — Red Canyon — on the R1T that sparked it all.

The roasting moved indoors, but the spirit stayed the same. We source single origin beans from African highlands and Hawaiian volcanic soil, roast in small batches, and serve at every Rangeway Basecamp across the country.
