Highland Coffees
Ethiopia
Yirgacheffe
From the birthplace of coffee itself. Yirgacheffe beans are grown at high altitude in the lush forests of southern Ethiopia, where wild coffee trees still grow. Our washed process lot delivers a clean, vibrant cup with unmistakable floral character.
Region
Gedeo Zone, SNNPR
Altitude
1,750 – 2,200m
Process
Washed
Tasting Notes

Kenya
Nyeri AA
From the fertile slopes of Mt. Kenya, Nyeri AA is graded at the top of Kenya's rigorous classification system. These large, dense beans produce a bold, complex cup with the signature Kenyan brightness that coffee lovers seek out.
Region
Nyeri County, Central
Altitude
1,600 – 1,900m
Process
Washed
Tasting Notes

Hawai'i
Volcanic Soil Coffees
Big Island, Hawai'i
Kona Extra Fancy
Grown on the western slopes of Mauna Loa, in the narrow Kona Coffee Belt where morning sun, afternoon clouds, and volcanic mineral-rich soil create ideal conditions. Extra Fancy is the highest grade — the largest, most flawless beans from the harvest.
Region
Kona District, Big Island
Altitude
200 – 900m
Process
Washed / Sun-dried
Tasting Notes
Caramel, macadamia, cocoa
Shop — $38
Our Approach

Sourced with care.
Roasted with intention.

We work with importers who maintain direct relationships with farming cooperatives and estates. Every origin is selected for quality and character — we taste dozens to find the few that make it into our rotation. Small batch roasting means we can pay attention to each lot and bring out what makes it special.
