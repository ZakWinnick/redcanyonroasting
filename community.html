Our Communities
Who We Roll With
Red Canyon was born from the EV community. These are our people.
Bay Area Rivian Club
Pour-over pop-ups at monthly Rivian owner meetups. We set up at trailheads, parking lots, and anywhere the club gathers. Fresh-brewed single origins, good conversations, and the open road ahead. Red Canyon is the unofficial (but kind of official) coffee of the Bay Area Rivian community.
NorCal EVs
Serving the broader Northern California EV community at rallies, group drives, and charging events. Whether it's a Tesla meetup, a Rivian run, or a multi-brand gathering, we show up with beans, a grinder, and good energy. The EV community runs on coffee — we make sure it's good coffee.
