Our Pledge

What we put our
name on.

We're a small company. That's not an excuse. It's a reason to get this right from the start.

Every decision we make touches someone's land, labor, or morning. We take that seriously. These aren't goals on a roadmap. They're how we operate now.

Sourcing

We buy single origin from farming cooperatives that practice sustainable cultivation. We pay above market rate because the coffee is worth it and so is the relationship.

Roasting

Small batch roasting means less waste. We roast what we sell, not what we hope to sell. When a lot doesn't meet our standard, we adjust the process, not the volume.

Packaging

Our bags are compostable. Our shipping materials are recyclable. We're not perfect here yet, but we're not pretending we are.

Energy

Where we control the energy source, we use clean power. At Rangeway Basecamps, roasting runs alongside an EV charging network designed around sustainability from day one.

Pricing

We pay above Fair Trade minimums for every lot we source. Farmers who grow exceptional coffee should be paid like it.

Giving Back

A percentage of every bag sold goes back to the farming communities we source from. We'll share specifics as the program matures, not before.

We'd rather be honest about where we are than loud about where we're going.
