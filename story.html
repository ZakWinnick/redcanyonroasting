Our Story

Born from the
open road.

The name comes from a Rivian paint color. The coffee comes from a belief that the best things in life are simple, intentional, and worth the drive.

It started with a truck and a question. What if you could roast coffee anywhere — powered by nothing but the vehicle you drove there in? The truck was a Rivian R1T, finished in a color called Red Canyon. Deep, earthy red. The kind of color that looks like it belongs in the desert at golden hour. That truck, and that color, gave us our name.

The original idea was simple: mount a coffee roaster in the truck bed, plug it into the onboard power, and roast beans at trailheads, meetups, and charging stops. Coffee roasted where you stood, powered by the road that brought you there. It was a romantic idea. And like most romantic ideas, it evolved.

The roasting moved indoors. The beans got better. The process got more intentional. But the spirit — that restless, open-road, what's-around-the-next-bend energy — that never changed.

What we roast.

We source single origin beans from two regions we love: the highlands of East Africa and the volcanic soils of Hawai'i. Ethiopian Yirgacheffe with its bright citrus and jasmine. Kenyan AA with blackcurrant punch. Kona Extra Fancy, smooth as a Pacific morning. Every origin has a story, and we let the beans tell it — roasting just enough to reveal character, never so much that we bury it.

We roast in small batches. Not because it's trendy, but because it's the only way to pay attention. Every roast is a decision. Every bag is a snapshot of a specific place, a specific altitude, a specific moment in the harvest. That's what single origin means to us.

Where you'll find us.

Red Canyon is the exclusive coffee roaster for all Rangeway Basecamps — premium EV charging destinations where the journey is just as important as the stop. At every Basecamp café, you'll find our beans brewed fresh. It's the coffee you drink while the road ahead charges up.

We're also out in the wild. You'll find us pouring at Bay Area Rivian Club meetups, NorCal EV rallies, and anywhere the community gathers. If there's a Rivian in the parking lot and the smell of fresh coffee in the air, there's a good chance it's us.

Why it matters.

We believe coffee should be good — not complicated. We don't use jargon where a plain word works. We don't chase certifications for the sake of labels. We find great beans, roast them with care, and put them in your hands. That's it.

Red Canyon Roasting Company is a coffee brand for people who'd rather be outside. Who'd rather take the long way. Who believe the best cup of coffee is the one you're drinking right now, wherever you happen to be.
